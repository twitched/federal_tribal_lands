This zip file contains the official boundaries for all wilderness areas in the National Wilderness Preservation System. The individual boundaries contained within this dataset have been obtained from the federal wilderness management agencies (Bureau of Land Management, Fish and Wildlife Service, Forest Service and National Park Service).

This data is current as of the date contained in the shapefile name (Wilderness_Areas_mmddyy). 

In addition to the required metadata, the WildernessUpdates.xls spreadsheet provides feature-level contact information and update history for all polygons. For information about a specific polygon, use the contact information or source provided in the spreadsheet for the most current date.
